<span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-105">預測性維護 (PdM) 會預測維護需求，以避免與未排定之停機關聯的成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-105">Predictive Maintenance (PdM) anticipates maintenance needs to avoid costs associated with unscheduled downtime.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-106">透過連結裝置並監視裝置產生的資料，您可以找出導致潛在故障問題的模式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-106">By connecting to devices and monitoring the data the devices produce, you can identify patterns that lead to potential problems or failures.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-107">您接著可以使用這些見解在問題發生之前即予以解決。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-107">You can then use these insights to address issues before they happen.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-108">這個預測設備或資產需要維護之時間的能力可讓您最大化設備生命週期並最小化停機時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-108">This ability to predict when equipment or assets need maintenance allows you to optimize equipment lifetime and minimize downtime.</span></span>

## <a name="maintenance-strategies"></a><span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-120">維護策略</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-120">Maintenance Strategies</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-121">在製造業的歷史中，發展出數個維護策略。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-121">Over the history of the manufacturing industry, several maintenance strategies emerged.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-122">「反應式維護」可在問題發生之後予以修正。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-122">Reactive maintenance fixes issues after they occur.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-123">「預防性維護」會依照根據先前故障經驗建立的的維護排程，在問題發生之前即予以修正。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-123">Preventive maintenance fixes issues before they occur by following a maintenance schedule based on prior failure experience.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-124">PdM 也可以在問題發生之前即予以修正，但請考慮設備的實際使用，而非依照固定排程工作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-124">PdM also fixes issues before they occur but considers the actual utilization of the equipment instead of working from a fixed schedule.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-125">在三者之中，由於資料收集、處理與資料視覺化之故，PdM 是最難達成的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-125">Of the three, PdM was the most difficult to achieve because of historical limitations on data collection, processing, and data visualization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-126">讓我們深入看看看看每個選項。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-126">Let&#39;s look at each option in a bit more detail.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-127">「反應式維護」奉行「沒壞就不修理」原則。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-127">Reactive maintenance embodies the &quot;if it isn&#39;t broken, don&#39;t fix it&quot; mentality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-128">只要在資產損壞時才予以修復。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-128">Service the asset only when it fails.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-129">例如，您的 5 軸 CNC 加工中心機馬達只會在壞掉時才會進行修理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-129">For example, the motor of your 5-axis CNC Machining Center gets serviced only when it stops working.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-130">「反應式維護」會最大化元件的生命週期，雖然元件終究會故障。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-130">Reactive maintenance maximizes the lifetime of the component which eventually fails.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-131">「反應式維護」也會有未知的停機時間、正常元件由於受故障元件影響的非預期附隨損害，以及其他問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-131">Reactive maintenance also introduces unknown amounts of downtime, unexpected collateral damage to components harmed by the failing component, and other issues.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-132">「預防性維護」要求您根據預先定義的間隔來維護資產。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-132">Preventive maintenance requires you to the service the asset at pre-determined intervals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-133">此間隔通常是以資產的故障頻率歷史記錄為基礎。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-133">The interval is typically based on the experienced failure frequency history for the asset.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-134">這些間隔是以歷史表現、模擬、統計式模型建構等為基礎。此策略的優點是它可以提高運作時間、降低故障次數，因此能讓您排定維護計畫。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-134">These intervals are based on historical performance, simulations, statistical modeling, etc. The advantage of this strategy is that it increases uptime, results in less failures, and maintenance can be planned.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-135">在許多案例中，缺點是資產上更換的元件可能都還可以使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-135">The downside in many cases is the replaced component on the asset may have had some life left.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-136">這會導致過度維護與浪費。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-136">This results in over-maintenance and waste.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-137">另一方面，某些零件可能也會在排定的維護時間之前故障。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-137">On the flip side, some parts may still fail before the scheduled maintenance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-138">您或許已經熟知預防性維護：在每個設定的作業時數 (或一些其他計量) 之後，停止使用機器並檢查機器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-138">You probably know preventive maintenance well: after every set hours of operation (or some other metric), stop using the machine and inspect the machine.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-139">更換即將需要更換的每個零件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-139">Replace any parts which are due to be replaced.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-140">「預測性維護」會使用模型來預測資產可能會遇到元件故障的時間，以監視資產的使用狀況。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-140">Predictive maintenance monitors the usage of assets using models to predict when an asset will likely experience a component failure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-141">接著系統會為該元件排定它自己的「Just-In-Time 維護」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-141">That component then has its maintenance scheduled for &quot;just-in-time maintenance&quot;.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-142">PdM 透過同時最大化運作時間與資產生命週期，在先前的策略上進行改進。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-142">PdM improves on the previous strategies by maximining both uptime and asset life.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-143">既然您是在快接近設備的元件最大生命週期時維護零件，您花比較少的錢來更換可運作的零件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-143">Since you service the equipment close to the component maximum lifetimes, you spend less money on replacing working parts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-144">缺點是 PdM 的 Just-In-Time 本質比較難執行，因為它需要更多更具回應性與彈性的服務組織。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-144">The downside is that the just-in-time nature of PdM is more difficult to execute since it requires a more responsive and flexible Services organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ecd1e-145">回到 5 軸 CNC 加工中心機的馬達，若預測性模型表示馬達在接下來 24 小時內有 75% 的可能性會故障 (以來自機器中之感應器的資訊為基礎)，您會排定合理的維護排程 (亦即，以已規劃方式，而且不會對生產造成影響)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecd1e-145">Back to the motor of the 5-axis CNC Machining, you would schedule its maintenance &quot;gracefully&quot; (i.e., in a planned fashion, without disrupting production) if a predictive model predicts that the motor has, say, a 75% probability of failure in the next 24 hours (based on the information coming from sensors in the machine).</span></span>
